			Pigeon pea (Arhar)
Fertilizer requirement
	kg/ha 	kg/bigha
Urea	 32 	4
SSP 	220 	35
MOP	 - 	-


Time of Application of Fertilizers:
One weeding is to be done at 20-25 days after sowing.

Plant Protection Measures Against Diseases :
Wilt: The pathogen inhabits in the soil and perpetuates. Rogueing
and burning of diseased plants partly help in controlling the
disease.
