Mode of Application of Fertilizers:
The entire quantity of phosphatic and potassic fertilizers should
be applied at the time of final land preparation. Nitrogenous
fertilizer should be top dressed in two equal splits at 4-5 and 7-
8 weeks after emergence.


Plant Protection Measures Against Diseases :
For controlling jassids, two spraying at 15 days interval with
endosulfan (0.07%) or phosphamidon (0.05%) or dimethoate
(0.06%) should be given during the vegetative phase of crop.
