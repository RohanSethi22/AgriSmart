AgriSmart: Online Agriculture Expert

Crop Prediction

AgriSmart analyses the soil characterstics, geographical location and weather forecast to predict the crop that'll be most appropriate.

Crop Nurturing

Along with the crop to be grown, AgriSmart informs you about many general charactersstics of the crop, including which fertilizers are effective for it and in how much quantity should they be used.

Crop Protection

AgriSmart analyses images of your crops and uses a highly accurate machine learning algorithm to inform you if it has been infected by a disease, as well as informs you of possible treatments to protect them.
